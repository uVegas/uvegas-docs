# Troubleshooting & FAQ

{% hint style="danger" %}
This documentation is currently under development. The content for this page is still being collected and reviewed. In the meantime, please submit any questions or issues through our [contact form](https://uvegas.online/contact).
{% endhint %}

### Do I need strong knowledge of Unity or Mirror to use, customize, and publish uVegas?

Yes and no. Adding or modifying special features requires basic knowledge of Unity, C#, and Mirror networking. However, if you only want to give the existing functionality a new visual style, you can focus on the UI layer while keeping the underlying logic intact.

### Can someone answer my questions before I purchase?

Absolutely. The marketplace offers a wide variety of assets, and it is natural to have many questions before making a purchase. You are welcome to contact us through our website contact form or Discord channel, where we will gladly help clarify any product-related questions. We recommend not purchasing without first gathering the information you need.

### Can I learn something from uVegas products?

Yes, uVegas products can serve as learning resources, as is often the case with many assets. Our documentation explains the core concepts and key implementation aspects. However, this does not mean that our approach is the only or universally best solution—different problems may be solved in different ways.

### Can uVegas be expanded with new features or ideas?

Yes, we are always open to feedback and suggestions. If an idea improves the product’s quality or usability, we may integrate it into future versions so that all customers can benefit from the enhancement.

### What if I have a very simple or “silly” question?

There are no silly questions - only helpful answers. Feel free to reach out via our website or Discord channel, and we will respond professionally to any inquiry you may have.

### Who develops the uVegas products?

uVegas is developed in Germany by a solo developer with more than 20 years of experience in software development. You can find our [legal imprint](https://uvegas.online/imprint) (Impressum) on our website. In contrast to some non-European countries such as the USA, UK, Japan, Canada, or Australia, European regulations require us to disclose the party responsible for the product.
